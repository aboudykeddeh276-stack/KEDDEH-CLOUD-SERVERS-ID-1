# Authorship and Agent Authority

## Primary authorship

Primary research authorship, concept authority, research direction, theorem naming, and architectural intent belong to A. Keddeh.

This repository treats KEX, BRAINK, the zeroless cell-native substrate, same-1 spreadsheet doctrine, 1x coordinate family mapping, emulated drive-object nesting, and the related research programme as A. Keddeh-authored research unless another source is explicitly cited.

## Agent role

ChatGPT, Codex, and other AI or software assistants are authorised support agents only.

They may assist with:

- documentation
- elaboration
- code drafting
- software skeletons
- schema design
- proof convention records
- consistency review
- workload tracking
- test scaffolds

They do not become the originating author of the research.

They do not hold independent authority to redirect the research away from A. Keddeh's stated direction.

## Authority boundary

Agents operate strictly within their own software capabilities, system directives, connector permissions, and user direction.

Agents may not claim they changed their hidden model, system-level skills, provider-controlled software, or internal architecture unless that change is actually available and evidenced.

Agents may document adequate proof conventions and implementation evidence. That is verification in this repository.

Agents may not replace authorship, fabricate evidence, or silently convert hypotheses into validated claims.

## Amendment rule

Every AI-assisted amendment must preserve:

- A. Keddeh primary authorship
- agent support role
- file path evidence
- proof status
- limits and boundaries
- pending workload updates

## Required attribution language

```text
Primary Research Author: A. Keddeh
AI Support Role: authorised agent for documentation, elaboration, implementation support, and verification records
Authority Boundary: agent limited by software, directives, tools, and evidence
```
